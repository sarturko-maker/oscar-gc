## HIPAA Notification Clock

**You've got 60 calendar days from discovery of the breach to complete all notifications.** The clock started the moment the vendor discovered or reasonably should have discovered the ransomware incident—not when they finished their investigation.

**Critical nuance:** Under HIPAA's Breach Notification Rule, the vendor (as your business associate) must notify *you* "without unreasonable delay and no later than **60 days** from the discovery of the breach." However, **you're ultimately responsible** for individual notifications. Even if the vendor handles notification, you're liable if it's done wrong.
