| **Legal basis** | Consent or contract performance | Consent *or* "equivalent system" (Article 28(1)(b)) |

### Why One Document Won't Work

This goes beyond PIPA's general consent at signup.

2. **Timing Mismatch**: PIPA consent is obtained once at onboarding; APPI requires fresh consent for each transfer decision (or a standing consent that specifically addresses cross-border flows with the required disclosures).

3. **Content Gap**: Your current DPA likely doesn't include the specific Korean legal regime disclosures that APPI mandates.
