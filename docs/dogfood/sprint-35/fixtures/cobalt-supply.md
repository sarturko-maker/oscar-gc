# SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This Supply Agreement is made between Cobalt Manufacturing PLC ("Buyer") and Sterling
Components Pte Ltd ("Seller").

## 1. Definitions

"Applicable Law" means the law identified in Schedule 1 (Commercial Terms).

"Cap Reference Period" means the period of six (6) months ending on the date of the event
giving rise to the relevant claim.

## 8. Supply of Goods

Seller shall manufacture and supply the Goods in accordance with the Specification and each
Purchase Order accepted by Seller.

## 17. Limitation of Liability

Subject always to clause 17.3, the maximum aggregate liability of either party in respect
of all claims, whether arising in contract, tort, under statute or otherwise, shall be
limited to an amount equal to one hundred and twenty-five per cent (125%) of the total
charges paid or payable by the Buyer during the Cap Reference Period.

## 23. Change of Control

If any person acquires Control of the Seller, the Seller shall notify the Buyer in writing
within five (5) Business Days, and the Buyer may, at its option, terminate this Agreement
by notice in writing given within sixty (60) days of such notification.

## 31. Miscellaneous

(f) The construction, validity and performance of this Agreement, and all non-contractual
obligations arising out of or in connection with it, shall be governed by and determined in
accordance with the Applicable Law.

## Schedule 1 — Commercial Terms

Applicable Law: the laws of the Republic of Singapore.
