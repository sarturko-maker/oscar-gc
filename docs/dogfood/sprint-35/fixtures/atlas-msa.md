# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made between Atlas Logistics Ltd
("Customer") and Meridian Software Inc. ("Supplier").

## 1. Services

Supplier shall provide the software development and support services described in each
Statement of Work agreed between the parties.

## 2. Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of three
(3) years, renewing automatically for successive one-year terms unless either party gives
ninety (90) days' written notice of non-renewal.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Except in respect of fraud or death or personal injury caused by negligence, each party's
aggregate liability arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, whether in
contract, tort (including negligence) or otherwise, shall not exceed the total fees paid by
the Customer under this Agreement in the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise
to the claim.

## 14. Assignment and Change of Control

Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other. A
change of Control of the Supplier shall entitle the Customer to terminate this Agreement on
thirty (30) days' written notice, where "Control" has the meaning given in section 1124 of
the Corporation Tax Act 2010.

## 19. Governing Law

This Agreement and any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with it shall be
governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales, and the parties
submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
