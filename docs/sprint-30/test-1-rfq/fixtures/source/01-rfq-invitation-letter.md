# Request for Quotation — Indirect MRO and Industrial Consumables, 3-Year Framework

Pemberton Engineering Ltd. ("**Pemberton**") is conducting a competitive procurement exercise for the supply of indirect maintenance, repair and operations ("**MRO**") consumables and selected capital spares across our three UK manufacturing sites (Manchester, Sheffield, Bristol) and our two EU sites (Eindhoven NL, Lyon FR) for a three-year term commencing 1 January 2027 with an option to extend by mutual agreement for a further two years.

We are inviting Stanford Industrial Supply Co. ("**Bidder**") to submit a bid by **23:59 BST on Friday 19 June 2026**. Late submissions will not be considered.

## 1. Scope of the Arrangement

The successful Bidder will be appointed as one of up to two preferred suppliers under a non-exclusive framework for the categories set out in the Pricing Schedule (Document 03). Pemberton's indicative annual spend across the framework is **£18.4 million** (group total, prior twelve months). Pemberton does not commit to a minimum volume.

## 3. Scoring Methodology

Bids will be scored against the following weighted criteria:

- **Commercial (price and rebate structure)** — 45%
- **Service (OTIF, lead times, escalation)** — 25%
- **Compliance and ESG** — 15%
- **Contractual flexibility (extent of accepted MSA terms)** — 10%
- **Sustainability roadmap** — 5%

A score of less than 60% on Compliance and ESG, or material exceptions to the GTCs (Document 05) without robust alternative wording, will result in disqualification regardless of commercial position.

## 4. Order of Precedence

Where any conflict arises between the documents in this pack, the order of precedence shall be (i) these GTCs (Document 05), (ii) the MSA (Document 02), (iii) the SLA (Document 04), (iv) the Pricing Schedule (Document 03), and (v) any purchase order issued from time to time. The GTCs prevail in all cases.
