# 2. Ceiling Prices

For each SKU in Appendix A (the SKU master list — 1,247 SKUs), bidders must quote a unit ceiling price. The ceiling price represents the maximum the Bidder may charge during the first twelve (12) months of the Term, irrespective of actual market price movements.

# 3. Rebate Ladder

Bidders shall propose a rebate structure on the following ladder. Rebates accrue on aggregated group spend (across all UK and EU sites) per calendar quarter and are paid in arrears within thirty (30) days of quarter end by credit note.

| Tier | Quarterly aggregate spend (£) | Min. rebate % |
|------|-------------------------------|----------------|
| 1 | Up to 750,000 | 0.5% |
| 2 | 750,001 – 1,500,000 | 1.5% |
| 3 | 1,500,001 – 2,500,000 | 2.5% |
| 4 | Above 2,500,000 | 4.0% |

Bidders may propose higher rebate percentages or additional tiers in their bid response.

# 4. Volume Discount Triggers

Quarterly volume discounts above the rebate ladder apply automatically on the following category-level triggers:

- Category A spend exceeds £1.2m in a quarter: additional 1.0% on Category A
- Category C spend exceeds £1.0m in a quarter: additional 0.75% on Category C
- Category E spend exceeds £900k in a quarter: additional 1.0% on Category E

# 5. Currency and Payment

5.1 UK invoices in GBP. EU invoices in EUR at the local site's billing entity.

5.2 Foreign exchange risk is borne by the Supplier. No FX-related price adjustments are permitted within the first twelve (12) months.

5.3 Payment terms per MSA clause 6 (90 days from receipt of valid invoice).

# 6. Open-Book Costing for Top-50 SKUs

For the fifty (50) highest-spend SKUs as identified by Buyer from time to time, Supplier shall provide open-book costing comprising landed cost, Supplier margin, and any rebates received from upstream manufacturers. Buyer reserves the right to require margin compression where Supplier margin exceeds twelve percent (12%) on any open-book SKU.

# 7. Most-Favoured-Customer

Supplier represents that, for substantially comparable Goods supplied on substantially comparable terms, the prices quoted under this Pricing Schedule are no less favourable than those offered to any other customer of Supplier. Supplier shall promptly notify Buyer of any improvement in pricing offered to a third party and shall extend such improvement to Buyer.

# 8. Pricing Schedule Return Format

Bidders shall return Appendix A as an Excel workbook with the columns set out in the template. PDF returns will not be accepted. Bidders must also provide:

- A written justification for any SKU where the quoted ceiling price exceeds Buyer's internal benchmark by more than twenty percent (20%) (Buyer will share benchmark deltas during the clarification window with shortlisted bidders);
- An explanation of methodology for any FX, freight, or duty assumptions baked into landed cost;
- A statement of any sustainability premia included in pricing (e.g. low-carbon steel, recycled-content fasteners).
