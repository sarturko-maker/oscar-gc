## CRITICAL REDLINES

### 1. Clause 20.1 — Order of Precedence [BLOCKER]

- **Issue:** GTCs prevail over MSA. Pemberton can unilaterally amend GTCs post-signing.
- **Proposal:** MSA prevails over GTCs. Lock agreed terms for Term. No post-contract GTCs amendment.

---

### 2. Clause 10 — Indemnification

- **Issue:** Supplier indemnifies Buyer uncapped. No Buyer indemnity. Broad "any negligent act" trigger.
- **Proposal:** Mutual capped indemnities. Cap at 12-month fees. Remove "negligent act" from Supplier triggers.

---

### 3. Clauses 11.1–11.2 — Liability Caps

- **Issue:** Supplier: £18m max. Buyer: £100k max. 180:1 asymmetry.
- **Proposal:** 12-month mutual cap. Delete 11.2 or raise Buyer floor to £1m.

---

### 4. Clause 9.2 — IP Vesting

- **Issue:** All Foreground IP vests in Buyer automatically.
- **Proposal:** Joint ownership for jointly-created IP. Supplier retains sole ownership + licence for sole-created IP.

---

### 5. Clause 18.1 — Termination

- **Issue:** Buyer-only termination right. No volume commitment.
- **Proposal:** Reciprocal termination right OR minimum volume commitment (~£40m over 3 years).
